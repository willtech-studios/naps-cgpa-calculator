Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe and private?

Yes, your data is completely private and secure!

All your scores and course information are stored locally in your browser's storage (localStorage). This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No information is sent to any external servers
  • Only you can access your data on your own browser
  • Your data persists between sessions but is stored only on your device

This ensures maximum privacy and security for your academic records.

How is my GPA calculated?

GPA Calculation Formula:

GPA = Œ£(Grade Point √ó Unit Load) √∑ Œ£(Unit Load)

Grade Points:

  • A (70-100): 5 points
  • B (60-69): 4 points
  • C (50-59): 3 points
  • D (45-49): 2 points
  • E (40-44): 1 point
  • F (0-39): 0 points

Calculation Types:

  • Semester GPA: Calculated using only courses from that specific semester
  • Year GPA: Calculated using all courses from that academic year (both semesters)
  • Cumulative GPA: Calculated using all courses from multiple years (e.g., 1st + 2nd year)
What are elective courses and how do I add them?

Elective Courses:

Elective courses are additional courses you can take in your 3rd year. You can add:

  • 3-Unit Electives: Courses worth 3 credit units
  • 2-Unit Electives: Courses worth 2 credit units

How to Add Electives:

  • Go to any 3rd year semester
  • Click "Add 3 Unit Elective" or "Add 2 Unit Elective" button
  • Enter the course code (e.g., ECE 321)
  • Enter your score
  • The grade will be automatically calculated

Removing Electives:

Click the red "√ó" button next to any elective course to remove it.

How do I use the calculator effectively?

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Enter Scores: Click on any score field and enter your exam score (0-100)
  2. Auto-Grading: Grades are automatically calculated based on your scores
  3. Calculate GPA: Click "Calculate Semester GPA" to see your semester performance
  4. View Cumulative: Use the year-level buttons to see your cumulative GPA
  5. Add Electives: In 3rd year, add elective courses as needed
  6. Save Progress: Your data is automatically saved in your browser

Tips:

  • Enter scores as you receive them to track your progress
  • Use the cumulative GPA buttons to see your overall performance
  • Export your results as PDF for record keeping
  • Clear all data if you want to start fresh
What if I make a mistake or want to start over?

Correcting Mistakes:

  • Individual Scores: Simply click on any score field and change the value
  • Elective Courses: Click the red "√ó" button to remove unwanted electives
  • Recalculate: GPA updates automatically when you change scores

Starting Fresh:

  • Go to the "Actions" section at the bottom
  • Click "Clear All Data" button
  • Confirm the action when prompted
  • All scores, electives, and saved data will be removed

Note: The "Clear All Data" action cannot be undone, so make sure you want to start over before confirming.

Can I save or export my results?

Export Options:

  • PDF Export: Click "Export to PDF" in the Actions section to create a printable report
  • Automatic Saving: Your data is automatically saved in your browser's local storage
  • Data Persistence: Your scores remain available even after closing and reopening the browser

What's Included in Export:

  • All your course scores and grades
  • Semester and cumulative GPA calculations
  • Date of report generation
  • Professional formatting for printing or sharing

Privacy Note: The exported PDF contains only your academic data and is generated locally on your device.
